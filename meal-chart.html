Weekly Meal Plan

Pure-vegetarian · high-protein · no egg  |  cook for the 12 PM – 8 PM window only · phulka = roti without oil
Monday
Lunch
Masoor dal (1½ katori) + 2 phulka + turai sabzi + tomato-cucumber-onion salad + 1 bowl curd
4 PM
Roasted makhana + masala buttermilk (chaas)
Dinner
Paneer tikka (100g) + 1 phulka + green salad
Tuesday
Lunch
Kala chana (1½ katori) + 1 katori rice + bhindi sabzi + salad + curd
4 PM
1 fruit + 6–8 almonds
Dinner
Tofu bhurji (150g tofu) + 1 phulka + sautéed beans
Wednesday
Lunch
Lobia (black-eyed peas) curry + 2 phulka + mixed veg + salad + curd
4 PM
Moong sprouts chaat + green tea
Dinner
Palak paneer (100g paneer) + 1 phulka + salad
Thursday
Lunch
Toor dal + 2 phulka + cabbage-peas sabzi + salad + curd
4 PM
Hung curd / Greek yogurt bowl + few seeds
Dinner
Soya keema (50g soya) with veg + 1 phulka + salad
Friday
Lunch
Rajma (1½ katori) + 1 katori rice + lauki sabzi + salad + curd
4 PM
Roasted chana + green tea
Dinner
Paneer bhurji (100g) + 1 phulka + cucumber salad
Saturday
Lunch
Dal makhani (light, low cream) + 2 phulka + bhindi + salad + curd
4 PM
1 fruit + handful walnuts
Dinner
2 moong/besan cheela + mint chutney + salad
Sunday
Lunch
Chole (1½ katori) + 2 phulka + mixed veg + salad + curd
4 PM
Grilled paneer cubes (80g) or sprouts
Dinner
Mix veg + tofu/paneer (100g) + 1 phulka + big salad
Every day
• Half the plate = salad & vegetables
• No sugar in chai · no biscuits / namkeen
• ~100 g protein a day (dal, paneer, soya, curd)
• Drink 2.5–3 litres of water
• Nothing to eat before 12 noon or after 8 PM
Goal: 78 kg → 70 kg  ·  prepared with the RESET plan
