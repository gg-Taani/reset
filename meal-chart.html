Weekly Meal Plan

Pure-vegetarian · high-protein · no egg  |  cook for the 12 PM – 8 PM window only · phulka = roti without oil
Monday
Lunch
Moong dal (1½ katori) + 2 phulka + bhindi sabzi + cucumber-tomato salad + 1 bowl curd
4 PM
Roasted chana + buttermilk (chaas)
Dinner
Paneer bhurji (100g) + 1 phulka + green salad
Tuesday
Lunch
Rajma (1½ katori) + 1 katori rice + lauki sabzi + salad + curd
4 PM
Moong sprouts chaat + green tea
Dinner
Soya chunk curry (50g dry soya) + 1 phulka + sautéed veg
Wednesday
Lunch
Chole (1½ katori) + 2 phulka + mixed veg + salad + curd
4 PM
Handful almonds + walnuts + 1 fruit
Dinner
Grilled paneer or tofu tikka (100g) + 1 phulka + salad
Thursday
Lunch
Palak + toor dal + 2 phulka + bhindi sabzi + curd
4 PM
Hung curd / Greek yogurt bowl + few seeds
Dinner
Moong dal + vegetable khichdi (light) + 1 bowl curd
Friday
Lunch
Besan kadhi + 1 katori rice + sabzi + salad
4 PM
Roasted makhana + buttermilk (chaas)
Dinner
Paneer-capsicum sabzi (100g) + 1 phulka + salad
Saturday
Lunch
Mixed dal + 2 phulka + cabbage-peas sabzi + curd
4 PM
1 fruit + few peanuts
Dinner
Soya / veg pulao (small) + cucumber raita
Sunday
Lunch
Chana masala or rajma + 2 phulka + sabzi + salad + curd
4 PM
Sprouts chaat or a small home treat
Dinner
Grilled paneer (100g) + 1 phulka + big salad
Every day
• Half the plate = salad & vegetables
• No sugar in chai · no biscuits / namkeen
• ~100 g protein a day (dal, paneer, soya, curd)
• Drink 2.5–3 litres of water
• Nothing to eat before 12 noon or after 8 PM
Goal: 78 kg → 70 kg  ·  prepared with the RESET plan
