✅ Today's 6 wins

🍽 The 3 rules

  • Protein every meal — aim ~100 g/day. Keeps you full, saves muscle.
  • Half-plate veg/salad, ¼ protein, ¼ carbs. Halve the roti-rice.
  • Zero sugar in chai, no biscuit/namkeen. This is your #1 fix.
Target ≈ 1,600 kcal/day → about 0.5 kg/week → 8 kg in ~3.5–4 months. Steady wins.
